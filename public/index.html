FAQ

Do you follow a set curriculum?

No. Training is customized to your dog, your goals, and your lifestyle. We adjust lesson plans, exercises, and pacing as your dog progresses so you both keep moving forward.

Which areas do you serve?

I’m based in Fitchburg, Massachusetts and serve Worcester County (surrounding towns welcome). Virtual coaching is available anywhere.

What tools do you use?

We prioritize clear communication: rewards, markers, leash/long-line work, place, and structured routines. When appropriate, we may use additional tools to improve clarity and safety. Choices are made case-by-case with owner consent and a focus on fair, humane application.

How do you handle reactivity?

We emphasize state of mind, neutrality, and exposure at the right level. Dogs learn to disengage, hold position, and follow handler direction around triggers. We progress thoughtfully and proof in real-life contexts.
